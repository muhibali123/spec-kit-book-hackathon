# Further Reading

This document provides additional resources for learners who wish to deepen their understanding of Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics.

## Advanced Topics

### Machine Learning for Robotics
- "Robot Learning from Demonstration: A Task-Level Planning Perspective" by Argall et al.
- "Learning to Grasp under Uncertainty" by Fox et al.
- "Deep Reinforcement Learning for Robotic Manipulation" by Levine et al.

### Human-Robot Interaction
- "A Survey of Socially Interactive Robots" by Breazeal
- "Designing Sociable Robots" by Breazeal
- "Human-Robot Interaction: A Survey" by Goodrich and Schultz

### Control Systems
- "Feedback Systems: An Introduction for Scientists and Engineers" by Astrom and Murray
- "Robotics: Control, Sensing, Vision, and Intelligence" by Fu et al.
- "Adaptive Control of Robot Manipulators: A Unified Approach" by Sanner and Slotine
