## Key Concepts

- **Service Robotics**: Humanoid robots in hospitality, healthcare, and customer service
- **Industrial Applications**: Collaborative robots (cobots) working alongside humans
- **Healthcare Robotics**: Assistive and care robots for elderly and disabled individuals
- **Educational Robotics**: Robots as teaching aids and learning companions
- **Social Robotics**: Robots designed for human interaction and companionship

## Theory Summary

Humanoid robots are being deployed across numerous sectors, each with unique requirements and challenges. The human-like form factor provides advantages in environments designed for humans and in interactions requiring human-like behaviors.

Key application areas include:
- Healthcare and assistive care
- Manufacturing and industrial automation
- Customer service and hospitality
- Education and research
- Entertainment and social interaction
- Emergency response and disaster recovery

```python
# Example: Application categorization system
class RobotApplication:
    def __init__(self, sector, application, benefits, challenges):
        self.sector = sector
        self.application = application
        self.benefits = benefits
        self.challenges = challenges

    def display_impact(self):
        print(f"Sector: {self.sector}")
        print(f"Application: {self.application}")
        print("Benefits:")
        for benefit in self.benefits:
            print(f"  - {benefit}")
        print("Challenges:")
        for challenge in self.challenges:
            print(f"  - {challenge}")

# Example usage
healthcare_app = RobotApplication(
    "Healthcare",
    "Patient assistance and care",
    [
        "Reduced caregiver burden",
        "24/7 availability",
        "Consistent care quality",
        "Reduced infection risk"
    ],
    [
        "High cost",
        "Technical reliability",
        "Patient acceptance",
        "Regulatory compliance"
    ]
)
```
